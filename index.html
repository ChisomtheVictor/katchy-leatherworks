About Us

Welcome to Katchyway Leatherworks, a premier leather goods manufacturer based in Nigeria. Founded by Adam Inyang, we specialize in high-quality leather products including bags, wallets, belts, and accessories. Our mission is to create stylish, durable, and affordable leather goods that meet the needs of modern consumers, while promoting ethical and sustainable practices in leatherwork.

Our vision is to become a leading brand in the leather industry, renowned for our craftsmanship, innovation, and commitment to sustainability. We pride ourselves on our values of craftsmanship, quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, targeting professionals, fashion enthusiasts, and individuals who appreciate artisanal craftsmanship.
